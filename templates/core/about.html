Bio

I am a full-stack Django developer and energy systems specialist with a strong focus on modeling, optimization, and real-world energy market applications. My technical expertise spans Python, GAMS, Julia, and MATLAB, which I use to design and implement advanced optimization models and data-driven solutions.

I specialize in electricity trading and power procurement, with deep knowledge of power systems operation, control, and planning. My work sits at the intersection of energy and technology, where I develop intelligent systems to support decision-making in complex and uncertain environments.

My research and professional interests include renewable energy integration, machine learning applications for energy systems, and stochastic and distributed optimization techniques.
